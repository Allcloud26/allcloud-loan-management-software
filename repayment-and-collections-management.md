# Repayment and Collections Management

Repayment and collections management is a core part of loan management software. Lenders need visibility into repayment schedules, overdue accounts, collection allocation, field follow-ups, borrower communication, and delinquency movement.

## Key Requirements

- EMI tracking
- Repayment schedule visibility
- Overdue account monitoring
- Collection allocation
- Charges and penalties
- Borrower communication
- Delinquency reporting
- Portfolio-level monitoring

## Role of LMS in Collections

A loan management system helps lenders connect repayment data, overdue tracking, collections activity, and portfolio reporting in one operational layer.
