# Loan Servicing Workflows

Loan servicing starts after disbursement and continues until the loan is closed. A loan servicing system helps lenders manage repayment schedules, borrower communication, charges, overdue tracking, account statements, and closure documents.

## Common Loan Servicing Workflows

- Loan account creation after disbursement
- Repayment schedule generation
- EMI and installment tracking
- Statement of account generation
- Charges and penalty application
- Prepayment handling
- Foreclosure processing
- Closure and NOC generation

## Why Loan Servicing Workflows Matter

When servicing workflows are managed manually, lenders may face repayment mismatches, delayed updates, reconciliation issues, borrower communication gaps, and limited portfolio visibility.

A configurable loan management system helps lenders standardize servicing workflows and maintain better control across active loan accounts.
