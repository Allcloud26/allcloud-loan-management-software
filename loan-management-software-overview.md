# Loan Management Software Overview

Loan management software helps lending institutions manage the loan lifecycle after disbursement. It is used for repayment servicing, borrower account management, overdue tracking, collections, reconciliation, restructuring, foreclosure, reporting, and loan closure.

## Core Capabilities

- Loan account servicing
- Repayment schedule management
- EMI and interest tracking
- Borrower communication
- Delinquency tracking
- Collections allocation
- Reconciliation
- Reporting and portfolio monitoring

## Who Uses Loan Management Software?

Loan management software is used by NBFCs, MFIs, digital lenders, housing finance companies, vehicle finance companies, gold loan providers, and other lending institutions.
