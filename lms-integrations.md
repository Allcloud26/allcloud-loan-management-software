# LMS Integrations

Loan management software often needs to connect with customer portals, mobile applications, payment systems, communication channels, accounting systems, analytics tools, and partner systems.

## Common LMS Integration Areas

- Mobile applications
- Customer portals
- Payment gateways
- SMS, email, and WhatsApp communication
- Accounting and reconciliation systems
- Reporting and analytics tools
- Partner systems
- API-based integrations

## Why Integrations Matter

Lenders need loan servicing data to move across operational, financial, customer-facing, and reporting systems. API-based integrations help reduce manual effort and improve data consistency across the lending lifecycle.
